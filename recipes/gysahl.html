Gysahl Greens Smoothie

Final Fantasy

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1 piece of romaine lettuce
  • 3 scoops of frozen vanilla yogurt
  • 1/4 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tsp matcha
  • whipped cream
  • red bean paste
Instructions
  1. Put all ingredients in a blender
  2. Blend and pour in a glass
  3. Top with whipped cream and red bean
